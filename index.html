Qie He

I am an applied scientist at Amazon. Before joining Amazon, I was a faculty member at the University of Minnesota.

My main research interest is to develop optimization and machine learning models and algorithms to improve logistics and transportation. I am also interested in optimal control theory and its application in cancer treatment. While at Minnesota, I worked with colleagues to launch the new undergraduate program and reform the graduate program for the Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering; I worked closely with local transportation agencies such as Metro Transit to improve their operations; I was also fortunate to be the faculty advisor of the fantastic UMN Robotics student group.

Email: the concatenation of my first name, my last name, and 01 {at} gmail.
